Taco Recipe

A delicious Mexican taco recipe with seasoned beef and fresh toppings

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 packet taco seasoning mix
  • 12 small corn or flour tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped onions (optional)
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro (optional)
  • Sour cream and salsa for serving (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned and fully cooked, breaking it apart with a spoon as it cooks. Drain any excess fat.
  2. Add the taco seasoning mix and the amount of water specified on the seasoning packet (usually about 2/3 cup). Stir to combine and let it simmer for about 5 minutes until the sauce thickens.
  3. While the beef is cooking, warm the tortillas. You can do this by wrapping them in a damp paper towel and microwaving for about 30 seconds, or by heating them in a dry skillet over medium heat for about 30 seconds on each side.
  4. Assemble the tacos by placing a few spoonfuls of the seasoned beef onto each tortilla. Top with shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, shredded cheese, chopped onions, and cilantro as desired.
  5. Add a dollop of sour cream and salsa if you like.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy your delicious homemade tacos!
